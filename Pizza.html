The Origin of Pizza

Ancient Beginnings

Pizza traces back to ancient flatbreads in Greece and Egypt, topped with herbs and olive oil.

Modern Pizza

Modern pizza as we know it originated in Naples, Italy, in the 18th century.

How to Make Pizza

Making pizza starts with preparing the dough, followed by adding a rich tomato sauce and your favorite toppings. Finally, bake it in a wood-fired oven for the best flavor!

Popular Pizza Types

Margherita

A classic pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.

Pepperoni

A favorite among many, featuring spicy pepperoni slices.

Veggie

A healthy option loaded with fresh vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms.
